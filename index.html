About Cece

Cece is my beloved cat who my girlfriend and I have been caring for for over five years. When we first got her from the shelter, she was very shy and always had a little tilt in her head. Since then, she has become very friendly and trusting of new people.

Cece's favorite things:

  • tuna
  • sleeping
  • attention
  • music

    • Cece's early life

    Little is known about Cece's origins, but she was first discovered by my girlfriend Willow at a local shelter, going by the name Rose. With no hesitation, Willow brought her home in a white Chevy HHR that same day. Initially, she was very shy, always running away or hiding under the couch. It was around this time that her name was changed to Celia, or Cece for short, after the 1991 Slowdive song Celia's Dream.

    Cece's journey

    Since being adopted, Cece has been enjoying a happy and mostly healthy life. She has lived in three different homes. She has been cared for by many different people while my girlfriend and I were away on co-op. But most importantly, she has been loved by all who meet her.

    Cece today

    Today, Cece is a very trusting cat who is affectionate in her own way. While she does not like to be snuggled or picked up, she does like to be close to other people and she really enjoys having her head pet. Some of her favorite activities include eating, sleeping, meowing, making biscuits, and digging her claws into the furniture.
